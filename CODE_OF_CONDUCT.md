# Code of conduct

## Our Commitments

We strive to be an open and friendly community where people from all walks of life can collaborate and participate in the project, regardless of age, gender, race, ethnicity, sexual orientation, disability, or religion. We are committed to respecting all project participants.

## Expected Behavior

- Show respect to all project participants.
- Use constructive criticism.
- Accept disagreements while avoiding conflicts.
- Try to help others whenever possible.
- Discussions should be professional and focused on collaboration.
- Respect different opinions, perspectives, and experiences.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Use of sexualized or offensive language, as well as unwelcome sexual attention or harassment.
- Personal insults or attacks.
- Provocative, derogatory, or offensive comments.
- Public or private harassment.
- Publishing personal information without explicit permission.

## Enforcement

Any unacceptable behavior may be grounds for action by the project administrators. This may include warnings, temporary suspension, or a complete ban from participating in the project.

## Reporting Process

If you encounter problems or conflicts, please contact the project administration via email at [admin@stealthsurf.app]. We will review all complaints and take appropriate action.

## Scope

This Code of Conduct applies to all public interactions related to the project, including repositories, issues, pull requests, and any other communication channels used in the context of the project.

## Responsibility

Project administrators are responsible for enforcing this Code of Conduct and have the right to take action to ensure its compliance.
